About QR Link

zbar-wasm Powered

High-performance barcode scanning using a compiled C++ WebAssembly engine. Decodes instantly, even in low light.

100% Local & Private

Your camera feed and QR contents are processed strictly inside your browser. No data is ever sent to any external server.

Simple, Robust & FOSS

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) built using standard browser APIs. Designed to work anywhere, offline-first.
